How to Get Your TN Unarmed License Online:
3 Simple Steps

Getting your Tennessee guard card is a straightforward process. Follow these three steps to start your career as a professional security guard in Tennessee.

STEP 1

Verify Eligibility & Enroll

Confirm you meet all state requirements. Then, enroll in the mandated four-hour course for your Tennessee security certification.

STEP 2

Complete Online Training

Finish the self-paced course and pass the 50-question final exam. Receive your Training Certificate via email within 24 hours.

STEP 3

Apply to the State

Submit your application through the TN Department of Commerce and Insurance's CORE portal. Upload your Training Certificate, provide a passport-style photo, fingerprinting, and pay the state fees.

How Can You Avoid Application Delays?

To prevent delays, ensure your application information exactly matches your government ID. Double-check all details before submitting.
