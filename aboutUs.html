Alliance Training & Testing is the most reputable and trusted veteran-led five-star rated security guard license training in Tennessee. We provide Tennessee's best professional armed security license and unarmed security license certification training.

Angel Rodriguez CPP

Angel has more than 30 years of combined experience in military, corporate security and facility management operations. Angel started his career in the United States Army as an Infantryman. He served in the Gulf War with the 101st Airborne Division in Desert Shield and Desert Storm and was awarded the Bronze Star.

A member of the American Society of Industrial Security (ASIS), he holds the Certified Protection Professional (CPP) designation, recognized internationally as the top professional certification for security practitioners.

Angel is also certified as a Facility Management Professional, Physical Security Professional, Certified Protection Officer, FEMA certified in Emergency Management, Red Cross CPR/First Aid/AED instructor, and a Tennessee licensed security officer trainer. Angel currently serves on the Private Protective Services Committee with the Department of Commerce and was the primary author of the (4) Hour General Training Curriculum.
